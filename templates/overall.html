Scoring: Click on a name to see a scoring breakdown. In the group stage, for each team that you guessed in the correct order, I awarded 1 point. For each team correctly chosen to advance to the round of 16, I awarded 2 points. For each team correctly predicted to advance to the Ro8, 5 points, for each correct team in the semis, 7 points, each finalist 12 points, and for the champion 19 points. The scoring system is known as the "fibbonacci" method of scoring brackets - the amount of points awarded for a correct prediction in a round is equal to the sum of the 2 previous rounds. The motivation for this system comes from this source here: it was found after trawling through loads of march madness prediction data, the fibonnaci method ensures that each round has roughly equal weight towards the final score. Actually read the link if you have time, the methodology for determining this "optimal" scoring method is simple and convincing.
